Oncademy Email Templates Preview

Preview of automatically sent email templates

1. Course Enrollment Success Email
When to send: After successful Stripe payment
Recipient: Student who just purchased the course
2. Educator Application Approval Email
When to send: When admin approves educator application
Recipient: User approved as educator
3. Educator Application Rejection Email
When to send: When admin rejects educator application
Recipient: Rejected user
